Overview

Nestled along the top of the Manitou Hills, this charming home offers a serene retreat surrounded by nature and lookouts. Inside you're greeted with an open layout filled with natural light and breathtaking mountain views. The main level features a spacious living room, two comfortable bedrooms, and a beautiful full bath. The kitchen is a standout with its new Frigidaire Gallery appliances and a stunning brick wall, adding a touch of rustic elegance to the space. Upstairs, a large spacious bedroom awaits, complete with a full bath and two patios, perfect for outdoor relaxation and entertaining. The meticulously landscaped backyard showcases colorful floral bushes and majestic mature trees, with amazing mountain views including Garden of the Gods and City Views, creating a picturesque and tranquil oasis, there is a 10' x 13' building that can be used as art room, meditation or yoga retreat or additional storage space. Take advantage of the nearby hiking trails, the famous Manitou Incline, and the lively downtown area. Don't miss out on this remarkable home situated right in the heart of Manitou. Seller will install a closet and a door to the upstairs living space to complete a bedroom upon buyer request. All updates to the home have been permitted through the City of Manitou and Pikes Peak Regional Building. The roof is new with warranty.
